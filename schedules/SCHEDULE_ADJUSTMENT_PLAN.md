## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

**For React Coding Tests:**
1. **Time yourself** - 30 min for components, 2 hours for small apps
2. **Code without tutorials** - resist the urge to look things up
3. **Explain out loud** - practice talking through your code
4. **Focus on fundamentals** - hooks, state, props, component composition
5. **Handle edge cases** - empty states, errors, loading

**For Algorithm Tests:**
1. **Start Day 21** - don't rush, you'll catch up fast
2. **Focus on patterns** - not memorization
3. **Use all three platforms** - Baekjoon (Korean standard), Programmers (realistic), LeetCode (learning)
4. **Explain solutions** - write comments, talk through approach
5. **Review mistakes** - every wrong answer is a learning opportunity

**For Sleep:**
1. **11 PM cutoff** - better to sleep than cram
2. **Weekends OFF** - non-negotiable
3. **Wednesday Pilates** - keep this, it's crucial for mental health
4. **Quality over quantity** - well-rested 10 hours > exhausted 12 hours
