## Success Strategy

### Sustainable Intensity with Weekend Recovery:
- **Saturdays & Sundays**: Mandatory complete rest
- **Built-in weekday breaks**: Every 2-2.5 hours
- **3 meals daily**: Proper nutrition for brain performance
- **Late-night wind-down**: Final hour for review, not intense work
- **Sleep schedule**: 2 AM - 9 AM (7 hours minimum)

### Daily Habits:
- Morning planning (15 min)
- Evening review (60 min)
- Track progress daily in spreadsheet
- Adjust based on energy levels

### Warning Signs (Take extra rest if experiencing):
- Consistent difficulty focusing
- Physical exhaustion or headaches
- Declining code quality or more bugs
- Irritability or mood changes
- Sleep issues

### Remember:
- **Weekends are sacred** - no studying, no guilt
- This is sustainable because of mandatory rest days
- Consistency over 12 weeks > short bursts
- Your brain needs recovery to consolidate learning
- Weekend rest actually improves weekday productivity

### Weekly Rhythm:
- **Monday**: Fresh start, highest energy
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Peak productivity
- **Friday**: Finish strong, prepare for weekend
- **Saturday-Sunday**: Complete disconnect and recovery
